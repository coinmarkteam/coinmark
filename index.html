About Coinmark Token

CoinMark is the first reliable platform to find or promote brand new Coins and Tokens. Using $CMT as a utility token that works as credits to post on the website, we're creating a cost-efficient promotion ecosystem.

Unlike outdated platforms like Bitcointalk which are full of phishing links and generic forums, CoinMark offers a unique and enjoyable experience for both project creators and investors.

Our revolutionary approach helps projects with limited budgets promote themselves effectively, ensuring every good idea has a chance to flourish in the crypto space.

Fees are directly correlated to token price, and all $CMT spent on promotions goes directly to Liquidity, creating a sustainable ecosystem.
